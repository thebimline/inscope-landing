Many projects suffer from an information structure problem.

Valuable information is locked in PDF reports making it harder to action.
Project goals, due diligence risks and tasks are not systematised or tracked.
Construction receives a programme timeline, while the project and finance do not.
Unidentified information gaps lead to delays when they block start.
Performance does not easily feed back into planning and risk avoidance.
Spreadsheets, reports, emails and specialist tools rarely form a coherent view of the project.
Reports are assembled from fragmented project information with limited visibility of the underlying operational activity.

The result is more time spent managing information and less time managing the project.

Success is planned and managed.

The Information Worksite is the answer to the information structure problem. It encourages teams to define what matters, track it and understand its significance. It allows teams to combine the financial timeline and project programme into one delivery timeline to track how performance affects financial outcomes. It helps create the project roadmap and information infrastructure needed for success.

LABS can support project documents such as planning decision notices, building control plan checks, specifications, drawing registers, RFIs and meeting minutes.

InScope is built on Airtable and configured around structured project data, workflows and interfaces. AI is used where it helps turn documents into useful project records.

Your project data is held in the project system created for your use. Ownership, access and export arrangements can be agreed as part of the setup.

InScope uses Airtable’s platform permissions and access controls. User access can be limited by role, project and responsibility.

Editing rights depend on the user setup. Some users may only view information, while others can add updates, upload documents or manage records.

Project information can be exported, retained as a record or kept available as a read-only project history, depending on what the client needs.

Pricing depends on the number of projects, users, apps and setup requirements. InScope can start with a focused setup and expand as needed.
