Many projects operate through systems that struggle to maintain an accurate and up-to-date picture from start to finish.

◎ Spreadsheets, reports, emails and specialist tools rarely form a single, coherent view.
↗ Reporting is disconnected from operations. Teams rely on reports assembled from fragmented project information, rather than outputs produced directly from a single live operational system.
⌕ Performance does not easily feed back into planning and risk avoidance.

InScope provides a comprehensive suite of applications designed for the UK development market that covers the key aspects of project planning and delivery, and allows teams to combine the financial timeline and project programme into one delivery timeline.
